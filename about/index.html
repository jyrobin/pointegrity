About Pointegrity

Pointegrity is an independent software company that publishes privacy-first developer and prosumer tools. We build slowly, in public, and stay small on purpose.

What we do

We build software that helps individuals manage, move, and hold onto their personal data without handing it over to a platform. Our flagship product is Pouch, a personal data relay for capturing, keeping, and sharing digital content across devices. We also operate Bazaar, a curated storefront for tools worth paying for.

How we charge

Our products are paid from day one. No free tier, no ads, no mining of your content. We offer 14-day trials so you can see whether our tools fit your workflow before committing. Pricing is a single tier per product: $8/month or $72/year for Pouch, billed through Lemon Squeezy as our payment processor.

How customers find us

We reach customers through our website, technical writing, developer communities, and word of mouth from existing users. We don't run ads and we don't do cold outreach. If you're reading this, someone either linked you here or you found us while looking for something better.

Where we are

Pointegrity is based in Taiwan. We serve customers globally through subscription-based SaaS delivered over the web.

Why "Pointegrity"

The name is point + integrity — the commitment that every point of contact between you and our software respects your ownership of your data. That sounds grandiose for a software company, but it's also the measurable bar we hold ourselves to.
