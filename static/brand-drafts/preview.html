What pouch is

Pouch is a personal data relay. You capture something on one device; it's available on another. When you're done with it, it's gone. When you want it to last, it lasts — on your infrastructure, not ours.

Most of what people capture is in-flight, not at rest. The small portion that matters long-term deserves explicit, opt-in durability. That's the model. The rest is polish.
